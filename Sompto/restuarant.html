Our story

Iya's Kitchen started as a stall of four plastic tables in 2016. Word got around about the jollof, and the tables kept multiplying until we needed an actual roof.

We still cook the same way — fresh market run every morning, nothing sitting in a freezer, nothing rushed. If we run out of something, it's because we made it properly and it sold out.
